## Delivery Notes

- **Pace**: Fast but controlled. Breathe between sections, not during.
- **Gold card reveal**: Half-beat pause BEFORE the card flips. Anticipation sells.
- **"3 seconds" payout**: Point at the number on screen. Hold finger there.
- **Convergence line**: Deliver slower than surrounding text. Let it land.
- **Academic rigor**: One sentence, stated with confidence. You're not justifying.
- **Close**: Each sentence shorter than the last. Slow down progressively. Final line at half-speed. Eye contact. Silence after.
- **Total**: ~490 words at ~160 wpm = 3 minutes 4 seconds (buffer for pauses).
